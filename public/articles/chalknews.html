Chalk News

  • Soumyajith
  • Alumni

Chalk News is an initiative by a group of 2020 pass-outs of IIT Bhubaneswar. We are an online news portal exclusively focused towards covering updates, innovations, policies, evolutions in the educational system and the student body across the country in general, and showcase these achievements, which are seldom talked about in mainstream media. We are a little more than one month old, and we are working hard to populate the website with quality content, achieve diversity in the perspective of our team and essentially establish our signature in the process.

The co-founders of Chalk News have been active members of the student community during their time in the college, in various capacities, be it part of student council, the placement committee, or the student journalistic body etc. Through these experiences, we felt the need of an unbiased, unpoliced platform that would effectively represent the voice of today’s students, and the youth in general. We also envisioned the platform to be a source of regular and reliable updates of everything related to the education sector and the current happenings and trends of the same. We also felt the responsibility to showcase and celebrate innovations and achievements of students from colleges across the country, which seldom gets limelight from the media.

We cover the latest news in the educational sphere, we write about happenings in campuses across the country, we write about student innovations, student involvement in social activities, we highlight factors influencing students’ lives and related career opportunities. We also intend to move into social commentary to a certain extent and showcase the perception of the youth in India, the tech-savvy, informed and opinionated generation of the country.

We also have an editorial section where we invite experts who are formally or informally involved in this field, to write about their experiences and opinions. You can read a number of very informative editorials written by the likes of Dr. Debapratim Ghosh and Dr. Amrita Satapathy on the website. We also have a podcast named “Talk the walk”, which you can find on the site as well. Chalk News will be launching its weekly newsletter soon.

We would invite you all to follow us on our social media handles and give us your valuable feedback about the content. We would also invite you to write any article you deem suited that we’ll be happy to host. You can reach us at hello@chalknews.in.
